Pumpkin Pie

Description

healthy pumpkin pie recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 9-inch pie crust
  • 15 ounces pumpkin puree
  • 1 1/4 cups unsweetened almond milk or any milk of choice
  • 3/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice or cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon cornflour or cornstarch

Steps

  1. Preheat oven 350F
  2. Mix rest of ingredients together in saucepan until bubbling
  3. use hand mixer to blend everything together and pour into crust
  4. bake ~40min and cool
